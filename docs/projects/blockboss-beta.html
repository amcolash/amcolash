BlockBoss (Beta)

(2016-Present)

Google Play Store

Since September 2016, I have been working on a new beta game from PerBlue called Blockboss. It is a location-based and augmented reality RPG game. I am currently working on hero implementations (similar to DragonSoul) and improving the game engine itself. This includes adding in new functionality into the particle system engine, creating a 3d territory-based map and creating tooling for VFX artists to preview their effects on heroes with a setup similiar to in-game.
